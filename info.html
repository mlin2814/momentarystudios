About Me

Hello, my name is Matthew and I'm glad you're taking the time to read this. I'm an amateur photographer that enjoys taking photos that commemorate special occasions. I do this all in my free time and with my resources, and am not influenced by making this into a professional venture. My view is to record my experiences as best as I can, and to share my work with others so that they may enjoy it as well. Generally, my photos are of places I go to or cosplayers I meet at conventions. I always share photos with the cosplayers as they so rarely actually get to see their work directly from the photographer. I want to recognize their hard work and let them enjoy my perspective of their efforts. Any time I can capture the moment with my camera, I'll do my best to get the most out of it.
